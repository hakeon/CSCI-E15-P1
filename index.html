Brad took CSCI E-12 last spring, and a whole pot full of self-taught, MOOCs, and college courses for what seems like decades... but is really just a few years. In essence, he is old enough to seriously think about retirement - and realizing that he can't... So, if he has to work, he might as well do something fun, something interesting, something somewhat flexible in geography, and pays pretty well for the effort... and programming fits the bill.
